## **The Problem We Solve**

### **The Financial Inclusion Gap**
In our regions, traditional banking reaches only 35% of the population, leaving:
- **68%** of small businesses operating without formal credit
- **72%** of adults lacking access to savings accounts
- **85%** of land transactions occurring informally without legal protection

### **The Informal Economy Trap**
Most micro-entrepreneurs operate in cash economies characterized by:
- **Unpredictable cash flows** that don't fit traditional repayment schedules
- **No credit history** despite reliable business operations
- **Vulnerability to economic shocks** without safety nets
- **Limited growth potential** due to capital constraints

### **1. Dignity in Service**

Our model isn't aid‚Äîit's investment. We build financially sustainable partnerships where both institution and client prosper together.
